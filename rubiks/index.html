About
Built on top of Alexey's flowchart, with two small corrections noted in green inside Learn mode.

Two different motions: drag a sticker in the direction you want that piece to move — the layer that follows your drag turns, and snaps when you let go. The direction decides which layer turns, including the middle (slice) layers: grab an edge or centre piece and drag across the middle to turn the inner row or column. Drag empty space (or off the cube) to rotate the view and look around. Scroll to zoom; you can also click the face buttons or press the RLUDFB keys.
