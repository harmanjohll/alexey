This data comes from the Hipparcos space telescope, which measured precise distances to over 100,000 stars. The HR diagram was independently discovered by Ejnar Hertzsprung and Henry Norris Russell around 1910. The Main Sequence exists because of hydrogen fusion — stars on it are all burning hydrogen, just at different rates depending on their mass.
