Planets that never collide —
Orbital Mechanics.
Gravity is just one equation: F = GMm/r². You'll place planets around a star, set their velocities by dragging, and watch Newtonian orbits form — or fall apart. No calculus. Just forces computed as small velocity changes every frame.
