This model assumes each step is independent, each direction is equally likely, and all steps have the same size. Real random processes — Brownian motion of pollen grains, stock price fluctuations, diffusion of molecules — follow the same √N law when these conditions roughly hold. Deviations appear when steps are correlated or have heavy-tailed distributions.
