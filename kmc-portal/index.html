KMC PORTAL · 2D KINETIC MONTE CARLO
SiGe Thin Film Simulator
Faithful browser implementation of a 2D Kinetic Monte Carlo simulation for Silicon-Germanium thin film etching. Replicates the FORTRAN90 physics: desorption, surface diffusion, and bulk diffusion with Metropolis energy criterion on a full-scale lattice (1000×5000). Computation runs in a Web Worker — the UI stays responsive.
