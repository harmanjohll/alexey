Where do goals come from?
The xG Model.
Professional clubs use Expected Goals (xG) — a probability assigned to every shot based on where it was taken from. You'll build a geometric model, place shots on a real pitch, run 500 Monte Carlo simulations, and discover what the data actually says about scoring.
