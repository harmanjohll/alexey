2D Ising Model
Metropolis Monte Carlo simulation of a 2D square lattice of spins. Explore the ferromagnetic phase transition at Tc ≈ 2.269 J/kB. Watch magnetic order emerge and collapse as you change temperature.
Physics: Each site carries a spin ±1. Nearest-neighbor coupling H = −J Σ sisj. At low T, spins align (ferromagnet). At Tc ≈ 2.269, thermal fluctuations destroy long-range order. This is the simplest model showing a continuous phase transition with universal critical exponents.
