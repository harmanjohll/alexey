Where Do Goals Come From?
Professional clubs use Expected Goals (xG) — a probability assigned to every shot based on where it was taken from. Build a geometric and logistic model, place shots on a real pitch, run Monte Carlo simulations, explore real league data, and discover what the numbers actually say about scoring.
